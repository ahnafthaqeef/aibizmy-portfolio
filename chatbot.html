Architecture

How it works

A clean RAG pipeline -- document ingestion to accurate AI response in four steps.

1

Document Ingestion

Your content (PDFs, HTML, text, product specs) is parsed and split into semantic chunks optimised for retrieval.

2

Vector Embedding

Each chunk is converted into a vector embedding and stored in a vector database. This powers semantic search -- meaning-based, not keyword-based.

3

Retrieval

When a user asks a question, the pipeline finds the most relevant chunks from your data using similarity search.

4

Generation via Claude API

The retrieved context is passed to Claude alongside the user's question. Claude generates a grounded, accurate response -- never hallucinating beyond what your data contains.

Use Case

Demo: Proton X50

The chatbot was trained on the full Proton X50 vehicle specification -- features, variants, tech, pricing structure. Here is how it responds.

Live Demo Conversation

Proton X50 Spec Assistant

A car dealership chatbot trained on the complete X50 product page. Customers get accurate answers instantly -- without calling a salesperson.

Customer

What safety features does the Proton X50 have?

AI Assistant

The Proton X50 comes with 6 airbags, ABS with EBD, Electronic Stability Control (ESC), Hill Hold Assist, and a 360-degree camera on the Premium variant. The Flagship variant adds Autonomous Emergency Braking (AEB) and Lane Keep Assist (LKA) as standard.

Customer

Which variant should I get if I want the panoramic roof?

AI Assistant

The panoramic sunroof is available on the Premium and Flagship variants. If that is your priority, the Premium variant gives you the sunroof alongside most comfort features at a lower price point than the Flagship.
