Boss + Pekerja

Two views, one truth

Admin (boss) sees everything. Workers (pekerja) only see what they're assigned to. No more "saya tak tahu" excuses.

Admin · Boss

You see everything

  • Every order, every stage, every deadline
  • Assign orders to specific tailors
  • See workload per worker — who's busy, who's free
  • Track deposits paid vs outstanding
  • Add new orders with customer photo + measurements
  • Performance stats: orders completed, on-time rate, top customers
Pekerja · Worker

They see only their work

  • Only orders assigned to them — no clutter
  • Update status: Received → In Progress → Ready
  • See deadline + customer phone for follow-ups
  • Tap to view order photo + description
  • Cannot see other workers' orders or the boss view
  • Mobile-first — works on any phone, no app install
How it works

From paper to phone in 15 minutes

No installation, no training course, no IT person needed. Open in any phone browser, log in, start adding orders.

1

Login on Phone

Open jahit.aibizmy.com on your phone, log in with email or Google. Add to home screen — looks and feels like a real app.

2

Add Your Workers

Invite your tailors with their email. Each gets their own login. You stay admin, they're workers — different views, different access.

3

Tambah Tempahan

New customer? Snap a photo of the baju, fill name + phone + due date + price + work type. Assign to a worker. Done in 30 seconds.

4

Track to Pickup

Worker updates status as they progress. Customer asks "siap ke?" — you tap and check, no calling around. Order picked up? Mark complete.

FAQ

Soalan lazim

Saya tak pandai computer / not techy. Boleh guna ke?

Ya boleh. Kalau you boleh guna WhatsApp, you boleh guna ini. No installation. Open dalam phone browser, login, terus boleh add tempahan. I'll walk you through setup on a 15-minute video call.

Pekerja saya tak ada smartphone canggih. Macam mana?

Works on any phone with a browser — including older Android phones. The app is light. No 4GB RAM needed.

Data customer saya selamat?

Data stored on Supabase (the same backend used by thousands of apps worldwide), encrypted in transit and at rest. Each shop has separate data — your customers, your data, no one else can see it.

Apa beza dengan buku tempahan biasa?

Buku tempahan: handwritten, customer call you to ask status. Kedai Jahit Manager: customer phone open, you tap and answer in 5 seconds. Plus your workers know what to do without you briefing them every morning.
