How it works

From your business to a live phone agent

A clean four-step build — typically live within 2 weeks of you handing over the knowledge base.

1

Knowledge Base Capture

I interview you and document everything an agent needs: services, pricing, hours, staff, policies, FAQs, edge cases. Output: a structured KB doc you own forever.

2

Intent & Flow Design

Every common call is mapped to an intent (BookAppointment, FeeQuery, ReportAbsence, Complaint, etc.) with required slots and escalation triggers.

3

Voice Agent Build

Built on Retell AI with ElevenLabs voice (custom voice cloning available). Connected to your calendar, CRM, and SMS provider via webhooks.

4

Go-Live & Tuning

Soft-launch on a forwarded number for 1–2 weeks. I review recorded calls weekly, refine the prompts, fix edge cases, and hand over the dashboard.

FAQ

Common questions

Things business owners usually ask before signing up.

How much does this cost?

Setup is project-based depending on intent count and integration complexity. Monthly running costs start from approximately RM150–400/month (Retell + ElevenLabs + phone number) plus per-minute usage. Quoted exactly after the discovery call.

How long does it take to go live?

Typically 2 weeks from the day you hand over the knowledge base. Faster if your business is straightforward and slower if there are complex CRM integrations or multilingual flows.

Will it sound robotic?

No. ElevenLabs voices are indistinguishable from real receptionists for most callers. Custom voice cloning is also available — train it on your own staff's voice for a fully branded experience.

What happens when the agent doesn't know something?

It says so explicitly and offers options: take a message, transfer to a human, or schedule a callback. It never hallucinates pricing or makes up policy answers.

Does it work in Bahasa Malaysia?

Yes — fully bilingual. Demonstrated in the Damansara Children Academy case study, with the agent switching between English and BM mid-call based on caller language.

Can I review what the agent says before going live?

Yes. Soft-launch is mandatory — 1–2 weeks on a forwarded number where you and I both review every call. Prompts get refined until you're comfortable, then we cut over the main number.

Ready to stop missing calls?

15 minutes is all I need to scope your business and quote the build. No commitment.
